Tips for Proper Application of Car Ceramic Coating

Tips for Proper Application of Car Ceramic Coating


Applying car ceramic coating is a great way to protect your vehicle and keep it looking shiny for years! It's not so difficult if you follow the proper steps (and avoid making common mistakes). First off, make sure that your car is clean and free of any debris. A thorough wash with soap and water should do the trick. Once the car is clean, use a clay bar to remove any dirt or grime from the exterior surfaces.

Next, you'll want to prepare the surface for application of the ceramic coating. Start by using an alcohol-based prep solution to ensure that there is no residue left on the surface before applying the product. If necessary, use fine steel wool or a polishing compound to further prep the surface. After this step has been completed, you can now begin applying the ceramic coating.

(It's important) to note that most ceramic coatings require two layers in order for optimal protection and shine. Begin by spraying a light mist of product onto one section at a time and then immediately wipe off any excess with a microfiber cloth in a swirling motion. Allow each layer to dry completely before moving onto another section of your vehicle; usually about 30 minutes per layer will suffice. Additionally, be sure that all doors and windows are closed during application as this will help prevent dust particles from settling into wet coatings - this could cause serious damage!

Finally, once both layers have been applied (and allowed enough time to dry) you can now enjoy your new shiny ride! Make sure to maintain regular washing habits in order to keep dirt and debris away from your newly protected paint job. With these tips in mind (and proper upkeep), you can rest assured knowing that your car will look like new for years down the road!
