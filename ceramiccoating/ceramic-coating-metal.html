Frequently Asked Questions

What is car ceramic coating?
Car ceramic coating is a type of protective nano-coating applied to the exterior of a vehicle that contains silica and other materials, providing protection from corrosion, UV rays, dirt, and other contaminants.
How does car ceramic coating work?
Car ceramic coating works by creating an ultra-thin layer on the surface of the car that acts as a shield against environmental elements such as dirt, water, and UV rays. The coating also creates a slick surface which makes it easier to clean off dirt or grime with minimal effort.
What are the benefits of applying car ceramic coating?
Applying car ceramic coating can increase the life span of your vehicle’s paintwork by protecting it against scratches, stains, corrosion and UV damage. It can also make cleaning much easier since dirt will be less likely to stick to the slippery surface created by the coating.
Is there any downside to using car ceramic coatings?
Although there are some advantages to using car ceramic coatings they can be expensive and require professional application in order for them to be effective over time. Additionally they need regular maintenance in order for them to remain effective at protecting your vehicle’s paintwork from damage caused by environmental elements such as rain or dust particles.
Do I need special equipment in order to apply a car ceramic coating?
Yes, you will need specialized tools such as buffing pads and polishers in order to properly apply a car ceramic coating so it is advised that you seek out professional help if you do not have access or experience with these types of tools yourself.
