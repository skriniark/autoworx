There are a few different type of ceramic coatings available to choose from.

The first is a hydrophobic coating which creates a water-resistant barrier on the surface of your car. This helps keep dirt and grime off your vehicle while also making it easier to clean. It also gives it a glossy look that will last longer than traditional waxing or polishing. Another option is ceramic nano-coatings which provide even greater protection against scratches and fading due to UV rays as well as minor dents or chips on the exterior surfaces. Finally, infrared reflective (IRR) ceramics are designed to absorb heat from the sun's rays before they reach your vehicle's surfaces; this helps reduce interior temperature in hot climates where air conditioners can struggle to keep up with demand!
